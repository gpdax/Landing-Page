Thor information

Thor is a Norse god of thunder, strength, and protection.

Thor Curiosities.

Red-Haired and Bearded The original Thor in Norse myths had striking red hair and a full red beard, which matched his fiery temper. This differs from the common blonde, clean-shaven Marvel portrayal.

A Huge Appetite Thor was renowned for his gluttony, capable of eating an entire ox and drinking three casks of mead in one sitting during a wedding feast while disguised as the goddess Freyja.

Goat-Pulled Chariot Instead of flying with his hammer, the mythological Thor traveled in a chariot pulled by two magical goats named Tanngrisnir and Tanngnjóstr ("Toothgnasher" and "Toothgrinder").

Dressed in a Wedding Gown To retrieve his stolen hammer from a giant named Thrym, Thor had to disguise himself as a bride, complete with a wedding dress and veil, with Loki accompanying him as his "bridesmaid".
